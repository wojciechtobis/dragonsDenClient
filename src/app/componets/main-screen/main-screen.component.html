Binary Classification

Probability of malfunction in some period of time - in our example it is 20 cycles. One cycle is some unit of time dependant on process (second, minute, hour, shift, ect.).

Regression

Remaining lifetime. Four mathematical models predict machine's time to failure. Prediction is made based on the equipment (and similar equipments) history and current state.
